🫥 The Problem

Browser horror games have no payoff

  • ✗

    One-shot jump scares — scary for 30 seconds, forgotten in five minutes

  • ✗

    Bloated survival sims — hours of setup before anything frightening happens

  • ✗

    No win condition — players drift away because there's nothing to actually beat

"I want to be genuinely scared — and have something to show for surviving it."

🏚️ The Solution

A horror game with a real finish line

  • ✓

    100-night challenge arc — a defined, beatable goal players can earn and share

  • ✓

    Play instantly in-browser — no download, no barrier, instant dread

  • ✓

    Escalating ghost AI — keeps nights 1 through 100 progressively terrifying

The only browser horror game where surviving all 100 nights is a real achievement worth bragging about.
