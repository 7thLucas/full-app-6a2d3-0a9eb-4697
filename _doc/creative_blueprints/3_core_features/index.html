What's Inside

Core Features

👻 Horror Gamer 🏆 Challenge Runner
🌙

100-Night Challenge

A strict, defined arc — night 1 is eerie, night 100 is terrifying. Survive all of them to win.

👻

Escalating Ghost AI

More ghosts, smarter movement, new ghost types each phase — danger compounds relentlessly.

🏚️

House Fortification

Board windows, barricade doors, manage electricity and light sources to hold the line each night.

🔦

Day-Phase Scavenging

Use daylight to gather supplies. Ration carefully — nights are long and ghosts never sleep.

🌫️

Atmospheric Horror

Dark forest visuals, ambient sound design, fog, and slow-burn tension — minimal UI for full immersion.

📦

Resource Management

Scarcity is the engine of tension. Every supply decision has a downstream cost.

📊

Score & Streak Tracking

Nights survived is your score. Personal bests, streaks, and leaderboard ambition keep players returning.

🔄

Fast Retry Loop

Game over → back in action in seconds. The compulsion to beat your record is the core hook.
