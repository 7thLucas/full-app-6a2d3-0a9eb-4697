# Design Guidelines — Horror Kutty Satthan

## Color Palette
- Background: near-black (#020617, #0f172a)
- Primary accent: blood-red (#dc2626, #991b1b)
- Secondary: deep forest green (#14532d, #166534)
- Highlight: amber/moonlight (#d97706, #fbbf24)
- Text: white (#ffffff), slate-300 (#cbd5e1)

## Typography
- Headings: heavy/black weight, tight line-height — conveys dread
- Body: clean, readable — slate-300 on dark backgrounds
- Numbers (night counter): large, bold, prominent

## Visual Style
- Dark, fog-filled forest aesthetic
- Minimal UI — immersion is the priority
- Ghost encounters: translucent white/blue figures
- House interior: warm amber light vs. darkness outside
- Horror atmosphere: slow reveals, tension over jump scares

## Components
- Night counter: always visible, bold, top-center
- Resource bars: compact, dark-themed
- Action buttons: dark with red/amber accents
- Game over screen: stark, fast-retry prominent
- Victory screen: cinematic, shareable result

## Feel
Isolation, dread, compulsion to survive. Every UI decision reinforces the horror.
